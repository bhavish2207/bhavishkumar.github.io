Database Design, Management
& Warehousing

Data Visualization & Dashboarding

I have built several user interactive web applications (dashboards) on R Shiny as part of my client project at Mu Sigma and also as part of my academic projects. Text Mining & Natural Language Processing

I also got to learn and implement topic modeling algorithms such as LDA & NMF.
